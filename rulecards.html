Guidelines of the Game

Critter Roll is an improv-based card game where your creativity sets the limits.

This is a game about storytelling where imagination, fairness, fun, and respect are the cornerstones of good role-play. You are making your own characters through these critters, and they deserve the story you want them to be in.
