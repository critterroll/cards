Guidelines of the Game

Before you read the rules!

Critter Roll is a Role-Playing Game. And like many games in this genre, every game master, player, and campaign comes with its own house rules based on what everyone is comfortable with. Imagination, creativity, fair play, and respect are the cornerstones of good rol-eplay - with fun being the centerpiece. You are creating your own characters through these virtual critters, and they deserve the story you want them to be in. With that in mind, not all of these rules are enforced. In fact, casual play exists even in the Critter Roll Rule Cards. Take them as guidelines, and feel free to branch out in any direction you'd like. This means you don't have to follow the Story Modules or even use the Map Packs provided. They exist for you, not the other way around.
