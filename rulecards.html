Guidelines of the Game

Critter Roll is a Role-Playing Game. Every game master, player, and campaign comes with its own house rules based on what everyone is comfortable with. Imagination, fair play, fun, and respect are the cornerstones of good role-play. You are creating your own characters through these virtual critters, and they deserve the story you want them to be in. Because of this, not all of these rules are enforced. In fact, casual play exists even in the Critter Roll Rule Cards. Take them as guidelines, and feel free to branch out in any direction you'd like. This means you don't have to follow the Story Modules or even use the Map Packs provided (Read Rule Card 9 to see how you can play without Map Packs). They exist for you, not the other way around.
