Great for beginners. Their power lets them inspect any place on the map when in an unlocked room. The Lawnbird does this by flying up to see the map through a bird’s eye view.
But since the Lawnbird is a ground bird that can fly for only a little bit, they must return to their original spot after inspecting. Stat-wise, the Lawnbird is very well-rounded.
It leans more toward offensive skills rather than passive. If you’re new but like to take risks, this may be the critter for you.
